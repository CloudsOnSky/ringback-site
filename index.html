Questions practices ask

The honest answers.

Do you touch our patient records or scheduling system?

No. RingBack only sees the text conversation with the person who just called you. It has no access to your PMS, your calendar, or any patient files — it captures a caller's name, reason, and preferred times, then hands that to your front desk. Your team books the actual slot.

What if the caller asks whether they're texting a real person?

It tells the truth — that it's the practice's text assistant and a team member will follow up to confirm. It never pretends to be a specific named staff member. Honest by design, so it protects your reputation instead of risking it.

What happens with a dental emergency?

Severe pain, swelling, or trauma are flagged as urgent immediately. RingBack doesn't make an emergency caller shop for appointment times — it reassures them, tells them a team member will call right back, points to 911 if it's serious, and pushes that lead to the top for your front desk.

How hard is setup, and what do we need?

One short call. You forward only your unanswered calls to a number we give you — a setting your phone provider already has. No apps, no new hardware, no IT project. Most practices are live the same day.

Will it book appointments over our team's heads?

Never. RingBack captures and hands off — it collects what the patient needs and gives it to your front desk. Your team stays in full control of the calendar and confirms every appointment.

What does it cost to try?

Free for the first two weeks, no card required. If it hasn't caught you patients you'd otherwise have lost, you walk away — and it's month-to-month after that, cancel anytime.
